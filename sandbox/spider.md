# Spider-Man Overview

## Spider-Man Overview
Spider-Man is a superhero in American comic books published by Marvel Comics, created by writer-editor Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko. He first appeared in "Amazing Fantasy #15" in August 1962. Spider-Man is the secret identity of Peter Parker, who gains superhuman abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. His powers include superhuman strength, agility, the ability to cling to surfaces, and a "spider-sense" that warns him of danger. Peter initially misuses his powers but learns responsibility following the tragic death of his Uncle Ben.

### Key Characters
- **Peter Parker / Spider-Man**: Protagonist, raised by Aunt May and Uncle Ben.
- **Supporting Characters**: J. Jonah Jameson, Harry Osborn, Flash Thompson, Gwen Stacy, Mary Jane Watson, Black Cat.
- **Villains**: Doctor Octopus, Green Goblin, Venom.

Spider-Man has appeared in various media, including animated series, video games, and films. Notably, he has been portrayed by Tobey Maguire, Andrew Garfield, and Tom Holland in live-action films.
