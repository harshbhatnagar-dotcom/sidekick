## Plot Summary
The story is set in a near‑future where a deadly pathogen called the **Reaper Virus** forces the UK government to quarantine Scotland behind a massive wall. When the virus later resurfaces in London, political leaders dispatch a task‑force led by **Major Eden Sinclair** (Rhona Mitra) to the quarantined zone in hopes of locating a cure.

Inside the wall, Sinclair’s team discovers a feudal society ruled by a warlord named **Kane** (Ian McElhinney). They learn that the virus has mutated: the population has developed immunity, but no cure exists. The film follows Sinclair’s struggle to navigate this brutal, hybrid world of high‑tech weaponry and medieval combat while searching for a possible remedy.
